# Privacy statement — Auto New Tab

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

- Your site patterns and link rules are stored only in your browser's extension
  storage (`chrome.storage.sync`), which your browser may sync between your own
  signed-in devices. Nothing is ever sent to the extension's author or any
  third party.
- The extension makes no network requests of any kind.
- No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking.

The only page content the extension touches is the link you click: if your
rules say so, it adds `target="_blank"` and `rel="noopener"` to that link.
